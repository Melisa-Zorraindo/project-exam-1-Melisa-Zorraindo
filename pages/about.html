fit, fitter, fittest

Helping you become the best version of yourself

There’s a large group of people out there who feel they can’t be happy and accepted because they don’t think they have the bodies they’re “supposed to” have. Unfortunately, what they feel they are “supposed to” have is an ideal they see in magazines, on television, and on social media that isn’t attainable by 90% of us. My goal is to challenge these unrealistic ideals of physical attractiveness, build positive body image, and improve self-confidence by helping you build a strong and healthy body that you'll be proud to show off. Want to get started? In this blog I offer high-quality training and nutrition coaching to maximise your bodybuilding success. Here for the ladies (and gentlemen too) who want to build muscle and shred fat.

I began working out with my parents at the age of 6, and at the age of 13, I was a weightlifter. I trained professionally for 2 years, until I decided to pursue a career in modelling. My modelling career lasted 11 years and it was demanding, but very rewarding. In 2002 I took up bodybuilding and discovered that I love it. A year later I began competing. I got the first place on the WBFF in 2018 and 2019. Currently, I train ther women and help them achieve their goals, wheather they involve competing in world championships or just achieving a healthy relationship with food and their bodies in general.
